L7: System Integration, Validation, and Future Directions
================================================================

**Bridge:** From Components to Deployed System

Learning Objectives
-------------------

- Understand complete system architecture  
- Master validation and testing strategies  
- Learn safety assessment methods  
- Explore future trends and challenges  

Outline
-------

Part 1: System Architecture and Integration
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Covers the overall architecture of autonomous vehicles integrating perception, prediction, planning, control, and decision modules. Discusses middleware frameworks such as ROS 2 and data synchronization.

Part 2: Validation and Testing
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Explores simulation-based validation, hardware-in-the-loop (HIL), and on-road testing. Introduces test coverage, corner-case discovery, and scenario-based evaluation.

Part 3: Safety Standards and Certification
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Reviews safety and regulatory frameworks including ISO 26262, SOTIF, and UL 4600. Discusses certification challenges for AI-driven systems.

Part 4: Data and Continuous Learning
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Highlights the importance of data-driven updates and online learning. Covers fleet learning, data labeling pipelines, and domain adaptation.

Part 5: Future Trends and Open Challenges
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Discusses trends such as V2X communication, cooperative perception, end-to-end learning, and digital twin-based validation. Identifies ongoing research challenges in robustness, generalization, and explainability.
